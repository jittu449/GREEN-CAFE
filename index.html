About Us

Vikkys Green Caffeine Cafe was established in 2020 in Lakshmipuram, Guntur. Our cafe offers a friendly environment with comfortable seating, board games, and a relaxing ambiance.

We serve fresh coffee, delicious pizzas, pasta, and a variety of snacks at affordable prices. The cafe is family-friendly and a great place to relax with friends or spend time with kids.
